Welcome to our Charity

Our objective is to make a platform to accept donations of unused medicines. Redistribute unused, unexpired, unopened drugs to medicine affordless persons. Make a healthy and happy nation. Decrease amount of medicine going to waste. Avoid cost of drug disposal.

"Your little effort can give others second chance to live life"

Our vision is to build a society where there shall be no medicine affordless persons and to redistribute unused, unexpired, unopened drugs to them
